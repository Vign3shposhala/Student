Learning Strategies

Active Recall

Test yourself frequently to reinforce learning and improve retention.

Tips:

  • Create practice questions
  • Use flashcards
  • Explain concepts without notes

Spaced Repetition

Review material at increasing intervals to optimize long-term retention.

Tips:

  • Use apps like Anki
  • Schedule reviews
  • Increase intervals between reviews

Project-Based Learning

Build projects to apply your knowledge and develop practical skills.

Tips:

  • Start with small projects
  • Solve real problems
  • Document your process

Teach Others

Explain concepts to others to solidify your understanding and identify gaps.

Tips:

  • Start a study group
  • Create tutorials
  • Answer questions online
